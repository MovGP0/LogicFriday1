## **About espresso and misII**

Programs *espresso* and *misII* are part of the Octtools package for IC design developed at the University of California, Berkeley. The executables distributed with Logic Friday are Windows ports of the originally Unix-based programs.

Both programs may be run from the Windows Command Prompt, allowing access to the full command sets. Certain commands, such as those that require Unix, and the *misII* `plot` command, do not work.

The programs, along with the original manual pages, are located in subfolders of the Logic Friday program folder, by default *c:\Program Files\Logic Friday*.
