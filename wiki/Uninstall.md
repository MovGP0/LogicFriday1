## Uninstallation

To uninstall Logic Friday:

1.  Click **Start**, then **Control Panel**, then **Add or Remove Programs**.
2.  Find *Logic Friday* in the list of programs and double-click it.

**Note**:\
The uninstall program will remove all files, registry entries, and icons originally installed by the setup program. However, it will not remove any files created by a user, such as logic function files (.lfcn) and image files.
